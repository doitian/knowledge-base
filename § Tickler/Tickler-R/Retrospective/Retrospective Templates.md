## Retrospective Templates

### Start, Stop, Continue

* What should the team start doing?
* What should the team stop doing?
* What should the team continue doing?

### Sailboat

* Goals
* Risks
* Helping team
* Delaying team

### 4 L's

* Learned
* Liked
* Loathed (Disliked)
* Longed for
